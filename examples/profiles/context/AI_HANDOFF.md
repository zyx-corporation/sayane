# AI Handoff

Review candidates before merge.

## Merge Policy

Inferred concepts should remain candidates until reviewed.

When merging context across systems or LLMs, prefer explicit uncertainty over silent assimilation.

## RDE Heuristic

Before merging, evaluate whether the change preserves the original intent, introduces an acceptable transformation, or creates suspicious drift.

## Candidate Concepts

- Context portability
- Explicit uncertainty in merge operations
- Sayane
- RDE evaluation
